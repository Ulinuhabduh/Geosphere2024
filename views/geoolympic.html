GeoOlympic (Earth Sciences)

"Enhancing Knowledge of the Geoscience and Oil Gas Industry for Youth"

Indonesia, as a country rich in natural resources, faces challenges related to energy security that require the application of high geoscience and cutting-edge technology in the oil and gas industry to solve. The application of geosciences in the industry is useful for detecting, describing and managing oil and gas reservoirs, enabling identification of resource potential, exploration and production planning, as well as efficient and sustainable management so that it is possible to solve problems related to energy security.
