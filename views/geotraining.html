Seismic Interpretation Training

Improve Practical Skills to Resolve Dynamic Challenges in the Upstream Oil and Gas Sector

Through this training and competition, it is expected to improve and evaluate students' practical skills in seismic interpretation, especially with the help of Petrel software. By attending the Seismic Interpretation Training (SIT), participants will practice operating Petrel in processing seismic data, including the process of identifying measurement results, interpreting geological patterns, and interpreting measurement results accurately
