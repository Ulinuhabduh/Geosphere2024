Seismic Interpretation Competition

"Beyond Boundaries: Seismic Exploration, Limitless Discoveries!"

Exploration and exploitation of oil and gas resources are key elements in maintaining global energy security. In the oil and gas industry, seismic interpretation is an integral part of finding, identifying and analyzing potential reservoir zones. Seismic interpretation allows geophysicists to understand the subsurface structure and make smarter decisions related to well drilling. The importance of seismic interpretation is increasing along with the complexity of oil and gas fields encountered. Efficiency in performing seismic interpretation is key to optimizing the exploration process, discovering new reservoirs and drilling more productive wells.
