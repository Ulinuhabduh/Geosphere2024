Seismic Interpretation Competition

Beyond Boundaries: Seismic Exploration, Limitless Discoveries!

Seismic Interpretation Competition is a thrilling journey into the world of geophysics, specifically focusing on seismic data processing. Teams will be presented with real-world seismic datasets, and participants will utilize their expertise to analyze and interpret this valuable information to unlock the Earth's geological secrets.

ü§ù Team-Based Competition

To foster collaboration and teamwork, the competition is structured with a team-based approach. Each team must consist of 3 members. Together, you will synergize your skills and knowledge to tackle the seismic challenges and vie for the coveted title of Geoolympic champions!

üíº Confidentiality of Data

We prioritize the utmost security and confidentiality of the data involved in the competition. Rest assured that all information shared and data provided during the Geoolympic: Seismic Interpretation will be handled with the utmost care and kept strictly confidential.

REQUIREMENT

For more details, please refer to the guidebook

The participants of SIC 2024 must fulfill the requirements below :

  • Participants are Undergraduate Students at university/equivalent level in Indonesia as proven by their Student Identity Card (KTM).
  • Participants are in teams consisting of 3 people (maximum) and come from the same university.
  • Participants can register via website according to the specified date and time.
  • Participants are required to complete the registration and registration fees set by the committee.
  • There are 2 stages in the Seismic Interpretation Competition, namely Preliminary and Final Round.
  • There will be 5 teams that qualify for the final round.
  • Collection of essays, full reports and PPTs using Indonesian.
